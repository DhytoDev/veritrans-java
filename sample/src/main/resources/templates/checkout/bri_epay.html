e-Pay BRI

  1. Please make sure that you have a User ID for BRI e-Pay and have registered for mTOKEN before going through with the payment.
  2. To obtain a User ID for BRI e-Pay, please register at the nearest BRI ATM.
  3. Please visit the nearest BRI branch office to register the mTOKEN.
  4. Payment via BRI e-Pay will be processed online and your BRI bank account balance will be deducted automatically based on your total amount.
  5. Your transaction will be cancelled if payment is not completed within 2 hours.
